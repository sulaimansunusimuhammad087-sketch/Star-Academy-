📘 About Star Academy

Star Academy is a community-based extra lesson program designed to prepare students for university admission. It runs every Saturday and Sunday from 8:30am to 6:00pm. Students are trained from Primary, Junior Secondary, and Senior Secondary levels. The academy focuses on Science and Arts departments to prepare students for higher education and professional careers.

🎯 Mission

To provide quality academic support that helps students pass examinations and gain admission into universities, while building strong academic discipline and excellence.

🌍 Vision

To become one of the leading community academic support centers producing successful professionals in science, technology, arts, and social sciences.

📌 Motto

Knowledge • Discipline • Success

💡 Developer Story

This system was built by a former student and current tutor of Star Academy. The goal is to solve a major problem: the academy was not properly recording student data, performance, or graduation records. This system helps: - Automatically generate student IDs - Store student records permanently - Track performance per term - Show pass/fail analytics - Support future academic decision making It is designed using knowledge from Data Science and Statistics to improve education management.
