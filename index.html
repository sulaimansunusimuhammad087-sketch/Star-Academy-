About Star Academy

← Back
Star Academy is a community-based extra lesson program designed to prepare students for university admission. It runs every Saturday and Sunday from 8:30am to 6:00pm. Students are trained from Primary, Junior Secondary, and Senior Secondary levels. The academy focuses on Science and Arts departments to prepare students for higher education and professional careers.

Mission

← Back
To provide quality academic support that helps students pass examinations and gain admission into universities, while building strong academic discipline and excellence.

Developer Info

← Back
This system was developed by Sulaiman Sunusi (Sir Mean). A student of Statistics and a data science enthusiast, former student and current tutor at Star Academy, and currently serving as an Examination Officer. Purpose of this system: - To solve the lack of proper student record keeping - To digitally manage student data and academic performance - To automatically generate student IDs - To track results, pass/fail rates, and academic progress - To provide data-driven insights for better decision making This project is part of applying data analysis and technology to improve education management in the community.
