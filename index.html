About Star Academy

Star Academy is a community-based extra lesson program designed to prepare students for university admission. It runs every Saturday and Sunday from 8:30am to 6:00pm. Students are trained from Primary, Junior Secondary, and Senior Secondary levels. The academy focuses on Science and Arts departments to prepare students for higher education and professional careers.

Mission

To provide quality academic support that helps students pass examinations and gain admission into universities, while building strong academic discipline and excellence.

Vision

To become a leading community academic support center producing successful professionals in science, technology, arts, and social sciences.

Motto

Learn Today • Lead Tomorrow

Developer Info

This system was developed by Sulaiman Sunusi Muhammad (Sir Mean). A student of Statistics at Bayero University Kano Level 200 and a data science enthusiast, former student and current tutor at Star Academy, and currently serving as an Examination Officer. Purpose of this system: - To solve the lack of proper student record keeping - To digitally manage student data and academic performance - To automatically generate student IDs - To track results, pass/fail rates, and academic progress - To provide data-driven insights for better decision making This project is part of applying data analysis and technology to improve education management in the community.
